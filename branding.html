Motion language
Amber glow breath
Interactive elements pulse softly with amber glow on hover. Slow — 0.3s ease-out. Nothing snaps.
Deliberate entrance
Elements rise 12px and fade in over 0.5s. No bounce, no spring. Calm and intentional.
Stroke draw
SVG elements draw themselves on load — tree growing, signature writing. Organic, not mechanical.
No glitch, no flash
Glitch effects belong to Leraniode/Action. Personal brand stays warm and precise. Not a machine — a person.
